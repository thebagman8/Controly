## What is Controly?

**Controly** turns your phone into a wireless remote control for your computer - no app installation required. Just open a webpage from your phone's browser, and you're set.

Perfect for a PC connected to your TV in the living room - control it from the couch without getting up for the keyboard.

## Features

- 🖥️ Turns your phone into a PC remote in seconds
- 🌐 No app installation needed - works right from the browser
- 🛋️ Designed for couch-to-TV use
- 💻 Compatible with Windows 10 (version 17763+) and Windows 11

## Installation

1. Download the latest release from the [Releases](../../releases) page
2. Run `Controly.exe` on the PC you want to control
3. Connect your phone to the same Wi-Fi network as the PC
4. Open the address shown on the PC from your phone's browser

> **Note:** Since the executable is not digitally code-signed, Windows SmartScreen or your antivirus may show a warning on first run. This is expected for independent apps not distributed through an official store - you can click "More info" then "Run anyway" (Windows Defender SmartScreen) to proceed.

## System Requirements

| Component | Requirement |
|---|---|
| OS | Windows 10 (17763+) / Windows 11 |

| Network | Phone and PC on the same Wi-Fi network |
